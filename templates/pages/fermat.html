Fermat's Little Theorem:

If p is a prime number and a is not divisible by p, then:

ap-1 ≡ 1 (mod p)

• Primality Testing • Modular Inverse Calculation • RSA Algorithm • Fast Modular Exponentiation

aφ(n) ≡ 1 (mod n)
