📚 About RSA

RSA (Rivest-Shamir-Adleman) is one of the first public-key cryptosystems and is widely used for secure data transmission.

Key Concepts:

• Asymmetric Encryption: Uses a pair of keys (public & private)

• Public Key: (e, n) - Used for encryption, can be shared

• Private Key: (d, n) - Used for decryption, must be kept secret

• Security: Based on the difficulty of factoring large prime numbers

📐 RSA Algorithm Steps

1
Select Primes

Choose two large prime numbers p and q

2
Calculate n

n = p × q (modulus)

3
Calculate φ(n)

φ(n) = (p-1) × (q-1)

4
Choose e

1 < e < φ(n), gcd(e, φ(n)) = 1

5
Calculate d

d = e⁻¹ mod φ(n)

6
Keys Ready!

Public: (e, n), Private: (d, n)
